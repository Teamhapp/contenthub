            Principles
            Design Rules
            blur_on

            The No-Line Rule

            Never use 1px solid borders to define sections. Boundaries must be established through background shifts, tonal transitions, and whitespace.

            auto_awesome

            Glass & Gradient

            Glassmorphism for floating elements (70% opacity + 20px blur). Primary CTAs use 135deg gradient from primary to primary-container.

            space_dashboard

            Asymmetric Balance

            Intentional asymmetry and expansive breathing room. Whitespace is a premium commodity. Large headlines with empty space feel expensive.

            layers

            Tonal Layering

            Avoid shadows for static elements. Use surface hierarchy (surface-low on surface) for natural lift. Ambient shadows only on hover states.

            block

            No Pure Black

            Always use on-surface (#151B29) for text. Maintains sophisticated slate depth. Reserve full rounding for small tags/chips only.

            tune

            Ghost Borders

            If a divider is mandatory, use outline-variant (#C9C4D7) at 15% opacity. Never 100% opaque lines. Glassmorphism edges at 10% opacity.
